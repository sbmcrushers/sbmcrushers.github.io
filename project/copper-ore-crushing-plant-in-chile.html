Copper Ore Crushing Plant in Chile

Mining set up a local branch office in Chile, which is one of the most important sales markets of Mining in South America. Each year, a large number of clients purchased crushing and powder-making machineries produced by Mining, and we have won trust from our clients because of the high-quality products and after-sales services.

Customer's Background:

In 2011, the clients contacted our sales person in Chile branch office. The clients wanted to set up a copper ore crushing plant with the following requirements:
Material: copper ore
Max. feed size: 900mm
Final product size: 0-6mm
Capacity: 100-120tph
