Ultrafine Powder Milling Plant in India

India is rich in various mineral resources and it is an important mineral processing market. With the development of local economy, lots of mineral processing plants are built there. Mining has built our oversea office in India for many years and we have numerous customers there, the market is quite mature.

Customer's Background:

Early of 2009, one customer from India got in touch with us, he told us that he was working in one mining company and his boss had one milling plant made up of Raymond mill and one primary 16"×10" jaw crusher. They would like to use this plant to grind calcium carbonate powder, but the final products could not meet their demand. They hoped that the final product size should be reach 5-10 micron and the capacity should be about 1tons per hour. Now they would like to built one that able to meet their requirements.
