150-200TPH Cobble Crushing Plant

Vietnam is an important mining export country in Asia, especially the exportation of Limestone, iron ore, coal, granite and bluestone products. With many years’ effort, Mining successfully developed Vietnam market, Mining crushing machineries and milling equipments are very popular in many provinces, like Yen Bai province, Ho Chi Minh City, Nghe An, Tỉnh Quảng Ninh and Hanoi. These mining equipments make up various mining crushing lines, sand making plants and mineral powder production bases.

Customer's Background:

We first met this customer, Dao ba ngoc, at our Vietnam oversea-office in 2011. It was his friend that told him the place. In that office, Dao ba ngoc clearly described his requirements and showed us the cobble sample. Then our engineers and staff went to his working site with him and drafted the cobble crushing plant design after many times discussion. During the time that this customer visited our head office, he witnessed the whole crusher production line and Mining machinery’s scale, finally paid the earnest.
